Geoffrey K. Pullum’s posts on the Lingua Franca blog (+ archive pages)

This is a reverse chronological listing of Geoffrey K. Pullum’s posts on Lingua Franca, a now discontinued group blog about language and writing in academe on the website of The Chronicle of Higher Education. His posts appeared on Lingua Franca once a week from August 2011 to December 2018. Unfortunately, The Chronicle has now stashed all of his Lingua Franca posts behind a paywall; if you are not a signed-up subscriber, you will be prompted to register with the site https://www.chronicle.com/; this is free, but only gets the right to view 2 articles per month without a paid subscription.

You can email him (pullum at gmail.com) about any of the posts you have read, but note that they are now frozen: He cannot alter them, no matter what errors, falsehoods, or calumnies they might contain. They must stand in their present flawed form until perhaps he decides to republish them, or until the day of judgment arrives, whichever is the sooner.
